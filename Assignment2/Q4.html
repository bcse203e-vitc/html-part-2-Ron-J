Egypt

Egypt, the land of ancient pharaohs, is famous for its iconic pyramids, the Sphinx, and the majestic Nile River. This country offers a captivating blend of history, culture, and breathtaking desert landscapes.

Tips

  • Dress modestly, especially when visiting religious or historic sites.
  • Always carry cash in local currency for small purchases or tips.

Netherlands

The Netherlands, known for its picturesque canals, windmills, and tulip fields, is a paradise for cyclists. Its vibrant cities like Amsterdam offer a rich blend of history and modern charm.

Tips

  • Rent a bike to explore cities and countryside like a local.
  • Book tickets in advance for popular attractions like the Anne Frank House.

USA

The USA, a vast and diverse country, offers everything from bustling cities like New York to natural wonders like the Grand Canyon. Each state has unique attractions and cultural experiences.

Tips

  • Check visa requirements and ensure your travel documents are up to date.
  • Plan for regional time differences and varying climates across states.
