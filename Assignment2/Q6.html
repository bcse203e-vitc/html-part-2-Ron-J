Delicious Pasta Recipe

Home Recipes Tips

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves

Steps

  1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and saut√© garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add canned tomatoes and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Mix the spaghetti with the sauce and serve with fresh basil.

Nutritional Info

Calories: 350 per serving

Protein: 10g

Carbs: 50g

Fat: 10g
